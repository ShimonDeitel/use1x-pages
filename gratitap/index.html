GratiTap ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

Data We Collect

The App collects only what is necessary to provide its core functionality:

You may request deletion of any data associated with your device at any time by contacting us. Because we collect no personally identifiable information, deletion requests are fulfilled by purging device-level anonymous identifiers from our aggregate logs.
